Air Freight Services

* Consolidations
* Cross Trade
* Part/Full Charter
* Customs

Close contact with our well established representatives ensures efficiency in delivery to the UK, USA, China and numerous countries from the Middle East and Asia with services tailored to the requirements of each individual customer at competitive rates.
