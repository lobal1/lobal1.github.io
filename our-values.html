Our Values      
   

We want to share and live by the following statements in all our businesses:

1. We are committed to providing a service to our customers, business partners and each other that we are proud of.

2. We are committed to treating people the way we would like to be treated ourselves – in an honest and fair way

3. We are committed to the long term, profitable future of our business and reflect this in the decisions we make

4. We will strive for excellence and be professional in everything we do – our own reputation and the reputation of the business is at stake

5. We encourage enterprise, recognise new ideas and always seek to improve the way we do things

6. We want to be a responsible member of the communities and environment we live and work in

7. We have respect for our colleagues – listening to them, being supportive and encouraging teamwork

8. We want our business to be an open, enjoyable place where talent and hard work are rewarded and people have the opportunity to achieve their personal goals
