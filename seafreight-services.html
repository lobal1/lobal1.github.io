Sea Freight Services      
   

Safe Hands Express provides ocean freight planning and consultation services and advice, to simplify your financial transactions. The selection of shiping lines, consolidation options, container specifications, packaging requirements and customs classification are additional areas in which our ocean export professionals provide valuable assistance.

We offer our clients the options of

  • LCL and FCL shipments

  • Transit Loads

  • Reefer Loads

  • Heavy Loads

  • Ro-Ro & Break Bulk Services

In addition we also offer specialized ocean services for out-of-gauge and over-size shipments. We employ an expert staff of logistics pro's to coordinate your ocean export or import programs.
